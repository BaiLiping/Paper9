Customizing the exported PDF

If you want to specify corresponding address, corresponding email, funding information, etc in the exported PDF, click Settings followed by Edit Macros. That will allow you to fill in information that will be printed in the PDF. Note for LaTeX users: via the Macros page you can also add custom packages, create aliases, and LaTeX newcommands.
Do you need an anonymized version of your exported document? Select Export followed by Export Options and pick the vendor style called Default: Wiley Blind
