Citations and References

You can cite bibliographic entries easily in Authorea. For example, here are a couple of citations \cite{Cavalleri_2016,Gregory_2015}. By default citations are formatted according to a format accepted by the journal. Here is one more citation \cite{Meskine_2019}. The bibliography in Authorea is generated automatically at the bottom of the document.
Do you need numbered references in the exported document? Select Export followed by Export Options and pick the vendor style called Default: Wiley Numeric
